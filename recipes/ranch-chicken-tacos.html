Ranch Chcicken Tacos

Description:

Ranch chicken tacos are a delicious twist on traditional tacos, featuring tender chicken seasoned with ranch dressing mix and served in warm tortillas with your favorite toppings. These tacos are easy to make and perfect for a quick weeknight dinner or a casual gathering with friends and family. The combination of zesty ranch flavor, juicy chicken, and fresh toppings creates a mouthwatering dish that will satisfy your cravings. Whether you prefer classic toppings like lettuce, cheese, and salsa or want to get creative with avocado, cilantro, and lime, these ranch chicken tacos are sure to be a hit!

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 (1 ounce) packet ranch dressing mix
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 8 (6-inch) flour tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup salsa
  • 1/4 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges

Steps:

  1. Place chicken breasts in a slow cooker.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together ranch dressing mix and water. Pour over chicken.
  3. Cover and cook on low for 6-8 hours or high for 3-4 hours, until chicken is tender and shreds easily.
  4. Shred chicken with two forks and stir to coat with ranch mixture.
  5. Warm tortillas according to package directions.
  6. Divide chicken evenly among tortillas.
  7. Top with lettuce, cheese, salsa, sour cream, and cilantro.
  8. Squeeze lime wedges over tacos before serving.
