Chicken Noodle Salad

Description:

Chicken noodle salad is a delicious and refreshing dish that combines tender chicken, cooked noodles, and a variety of fresh vegetables. It is typically dressed with a tangy vinaigrette or creamy dressing, adding a burst of flavor to the salad. This salad is perfect for a light lunch or dinner, and it can be customized with your favorite ingredients such as cherry tomatoes, cucumbers, and avocado. Enjoy this healthy and satisfying chicken noodle salad!

Whether you're looking for a quick and easy meal or a dish to impress your guests, chicken noodle salad is a great choice. The combination of flavors and textures makes it a crowd-pleaser, and it's also a versatile dish that can be adapted to suit your taste preferences. You can add additional ingredients like grilled chicken, crispy bacon, or crunchy nuts for extra flavor and crunch. Serve it as a main course or as a side dish, and enjoy the deliciousness of chicken noodle salad!

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces uncooked rotini pasta
  • 2 cups cooked, shredded chicken
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions. Drain and rinse with cold water.
  2. In a large bowl, combine cooked pasta, shredded chicken, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and parsley.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, Dijon mustard, garlic, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour dressing over pasta mixture and toss to coat.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 1 hour before serving to allow flavors to meld.
